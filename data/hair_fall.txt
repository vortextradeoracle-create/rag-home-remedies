Remedy Name: Hair Nourishment Oil for Hair Fall

Symptoms Covered: Hair thinning, scalp dryness, weak hair roots, excessive shedding.

Natural Ingredients: Cold-pressed Coconut Oil, Dry Amla (Indian Gooseberry), Hibiscus flowers and leaves.

Method and Preparation:
1. Pour half a cup of coconut oil into a small iron or steel pan.
2. Add 2 tablespoons of dried amla pieces and 3 to 4 fresh hibiscus flowers (or washed leaves).
3. Heat the oil on low heat for 8 to 10 minutes until the hibiscus leaves turn dark and the amla turns black.
4. Turn off the heat and let the oil cool completely.
5. Strain the oil into a glass bottle.
6. Gently massage this oil onto your scalp and hair roots in circular motions.
7. Leave it on for at least 1 hour (or overnight) before washing it off with a mild herbal shampoo. Repeat twice a week.
8. Amla is rich in Vitamin C and antioxidants that strengthen roots, while coconut oil penetrates deep to condition the hair shaft.

Safety Notes:
- Ensure the oil is not too hot before applying to the scalp.
- Wash hair thoroughly to prevent oil buildup, which can attract dirt and clog pores.
