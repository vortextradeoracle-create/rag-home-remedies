Remedy Name: Natural Support for Mild Fever

Symptoms Covered: Low-grade fever, body aches, hot skin, dehydration.

Natural Ingredients: Giloy powder or stems (Guduchi), Coriander seeds (Dhania), Water, Clean soft cloth.

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Giloy Decoction):
1. Boil one cup of water with half a teaspoon of Giloy powder or a small crushed Giloy stem.
2. Simmer for 5 minutes until the water is reduced by half.
3. Drink this decoction warm once a day. Giloy is highly regarded in Ayurveda for boosting immunity and managing chronic fevers.

Remedy B (Coriander Seed Tea):
1. Add one tablespoon of coriander seeds to one cup of boiling water.
2. Turn off the heat and let it steep for 10 minutes.
3. Strain and drink this tea warm. Coriander has natural cooling properties that help bring down internal body heat.

Remedy C (Cold Compress):
1. Dip a clean cotton cloth in cool (not ice-cold) water.
2. Squeeze out excess water and place the damp cloth on the forehead.
3. Replace the cloth every few minutes as it warms up to help draw heat away.

Safety Notes:
- Keep the room well-ventilated and dress in light, breathable clothes.
- Monitor temperature closely; seek medical attention if fever exceeds 101°F (38.3°C) or lasts more than 48 hours.
