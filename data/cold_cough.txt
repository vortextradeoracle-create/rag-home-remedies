Remedy Name: Traditional Treatment for Cold and Cough

Symptoms Covered: Runny nose, chest congestion, mild cough, sneezing.

Natural Ingredients: Ginger (Adrak), Holy Basil leaves (Tulsi), Honey (Shahad), Black Pepper (Kali Mirch).

Method and Preparation:
1. Boil one cup of water in a small pan.
2. Add a one-inch piece of crushed fresh ginger and 5 to 7 washed Tulsi leaves.
3. Let it simmer on low heat for 5 to 7 minutes until the water reduces slightly.
4. Strain the tea into a cup and let it cool until it is comfortably warm.
5. Stir in one teaspoon of raw organic honey and a pinch of freshly ground black pepper.
6. Drink this herbal tea twice a day (morning and evening) to soothe the airways and relieve congestion.

Safety Notes: 
- Never add honey to boiling hot water as it can destroy its beneficial enzymes.
- Not recommended for children under 1 year of age.
