Remedy Name: Sore Throat and Throat Infection Relief

Symptoms Covered: Itchy throat, pain while swallowing, dry cough, hoarse voice.

Natural Ingredients: Turmeric powder (Haldi), Warm Milk, Warm Water, Rock Salt (Sendha Namak).

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Turmeric Milk):
1. Heat one cup of milk until warm.
2. Stir in half a teaspoon of turmeric powder and a pinch of black pepper (which helps in turmeric absorption).
3. Sip this warm turmeric milk slowly before going to bed. Turmeric has natural anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties that help heal the throat.

Remedy B (Salt Water Gargle):
1. Mix half a teaspoon of rock salt in a glass of warm water.
2. Gargle with this warm saltwater for 30 to 60 seconds, then spit it out. Repeat 3 to 4 times a day.
3. Salt helps draw out moisture from inflamed tissues, reducing swelling and killing bacteria.

Safety Notes:
- Keep the gargle water comfortably warm; do not use hot water which can scald the throat.
