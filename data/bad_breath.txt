Remedy Name: Natural Breath Fresheners for Bad Breath

Symptoms Covered: Unpleasant odor from the mouth, dry mouth, plaque buildup.

Natural Ingredients: Green Cardamom (Elaichi), Fennel seeds (Saunf), Clove (Laung), Water.

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Chewing Spices):
1. Chew 1 to 2 green cardamom pods (Elaichi) after meals. You can chew the entire pod and seeds.
2. Alternatively, chew half a teaspoon of fennel seeds (Saunf).
3. These spices contain aromatic oils with natural antibacterial properties that help neutralize mouth odors and stimulate saliva production.

Remedy B (Cinnamon and Cardamom Rinse):
1. Boil half a teaspoon of cinnamon powder and 2 crushed green cardamoms in a cup of water.
2. Let it cool, strain, and use it as a mouthwash twice a day. Cinnamon has strong essential oils that inhibit odor-causing bacteria.

Safety Notes:
- Ensure daily dental hygiene (brushing twice and flossing) alongside home remedies.
