Remedy Name: Warm Nutmeg Milk for Insomnia

Symptoms Covered: Difficulty falling asleep, restless sleep, light sleep.

Natural Ingredients: Milk, Nutmeg powder (Jaiphal), Almonds.

Method and Preparation:
1. Boil one cup of milk (dairy or almond milk).
2. Stir in a very small pinch of nutmeg powder (less than 1/8th of a teaspoon).
3. If desired, add 3 to 4 ground almonds for added nutrients.
4. Drink this warm milk 30 to 45 minutes before going to bed.
5. Nutmeg acts as a natural calmative. In small doses, it helps stimulate the release of serotonin and melatonin, which relax the nervous system and induce deep, restful sleep.

Safety Notes:
- Never consume nutmeg in large quantities as it can be toxic. A tiny pinch is all that is needed.
- Establish a consistent sleep schedule and keep electronics away from the bed.
