Remedy Name: Natural Relief for Headaches

Symptoms Covered: Tension headache, sinus headache, throbbing temple pain.

Natural Ingredients: Ginger powder or Fresh Ginger, Peppermint Essential Oil, Lukewarm Water.

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Ginger Paste for Tension/Sinus Headache):
1. Mix one teaspoon of dry ginger powder (Sonth) with a few drops of lukewarm water to form a thick paste.
2. Apply this paste gently onto your forehead and temples.
3. Leave it on for 10 to 15 minutes, then rinse off with cool water. Ginger has mild analgesic properties that relieve localized tension.

Remedy B (Peppermint Oil Massage):
1. Dilute 2 drops of peppermint essential oil with a carrier oil like coconut or almond oil.
2. Gently massage the diluted oil onto your forehead, temples, and the back of your neck in circular motions. Peppermint's cooling effect helps relax tense muscles.

Safety Notes:
- Keep ginger paste away from your eyes to prevent burning.
- Ensure proper hydration, as mild dehydration is a very common trigger for headaches.
