Remedy Name: Immunity Boosting Herbal Decoction (Kadha)

Symptoms Covered: Low energy, frequent seasonal infections, fatigue.

Natural Ingredients: Tulsi leaves, Black Pepper, Ginger, Cinnamon, Cloves, Turmeric, Honey.

Method and Preparation:
1. Boil two cups of water in a pan.
2. Add a one-inch piece of crushed ginger, a small piece of cinnamon stick, 3 to 4 black peppercorns, 2 cloves, and 8 to 10 Tulsi leaves.
3. Simmer the water on low heat for 10 minutes until it reduces to about one cup.
4. Strain the liquid into a cup.
5. Add a pinch of turmeric powder and one teaspoon of honey.
6. Drink this herbal tea warm once a day, especially during seasonal transitions.
7. This mixture is packed with antioxidants, anti-inflammatory compounds, and immune-stimulating nutrients.

Safety Notes:
- Keep the recipe mild. Using too many spices can cause heat or acidity in the stomach.
