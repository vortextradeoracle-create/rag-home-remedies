Remedy Name: Natural Massage for Joint and Muscle Pain

Symptoms Covered: Knee pain, back ache, stiff joints, muscle soreness.

Natural Ingredients: Mustard Oil (Sarson ka Tel) or Sesame Oil (Til ka Tel), Garlic cloves (Lahsun), Turmeric powder (Haldi).

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Garlic Mustard Oil):
1. Heat 4 tablespoons of mustard oil in a small pan.
2. Peel and crush 3 to 4 garlic cloves and add them to the oil.
3. Heat on low until the garlic turns dark brown or black.
4. Let the oil cool until it is comfortably warm, then strain out the garlic.
5. Massage this warm garlic-mustard oil onto the painful joint for 10 to 15 minutes twice a day.
6. Garlic contains diallyl disulfide, an anti-inflammatory compound, and mustard oil promotes blood circulation, reducing stiffness.

Remedy B (Turmeric Milk Drink):
1. Drink a cup of warm turmeric milk daily. Turmeric acts as a natural systemic anti-inflammatory, easing arthritis pain from within.

Safety Notes:
- Do not apply heated oil directly to swollen joints if there is an active injury or open wound.
- Keep the massaged area warm; avoid exposure to cold air drafts immediately after massage.
