Remedy Name: Natural Relief for Acid Reflux and Heartburn

Symptoms Covered: Burning sensation in chest, sour burps, mild stomach irritation.

Natural Ingredients: Cold Milk, Fennel seeds (Saunf), Mint leaves (Pudina).

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Cold Milk):
1. Take half a glass of cold, unsweetened milk.
2. Sip it slowly. Cold milk has a high calcium content that helps neutralize excess hydrochloric acid in the stomach, providing immediate soothing relief.

Remedy B (Fennel Seeds):
1. Chew a teaspoon of fennel seeds (Saunf) slowly after meals.
2. Alternatively, soak a teaspoon of fennel seeds in hot water, filter, and drink. Fennel helps prevent gas formation and reduces stomach acidity.

Remedy C (Mint Water):
1. Boil 4 to 5 fresh mint leaves in a cup of water for 5 minutes.
2. Let it cool, strain, and drink. Mint reduces gas and relieves acid spasms.

Safety Notes:
- Avoid lying down immediately after eating to prevent acid backflow.
- Limit intake of spicy, oily, and highly acidic citrus foods.
