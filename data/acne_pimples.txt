Remedy Name: Natural Herbal Paste for Acne and Pimples

Symptoms Covered: Redness, inflamed pimples, oily skin breakouts, acne marks.

Natural Ingredients: Fresh Neem leaves or Neem powder, Sandalwood powder (Chandan), Turmeric powder (Haldi), Rosewater.

Method and Preparation:
1. Mix one teaspoon of neem powder (or grind a handful of fresh neem leaves into a fine paste) with half a teaspoon of sandalwood powder.
2. Add a pinch of turmeric powder and 1 to 2 tablespoons of rosewater to bind them into a smooth paste.
3. Apply this paste directly onto the affected acne spots or over your entire face as a face mask.
4. Let it dry for 15 to 20 minutes.
5. Wash off with cool water and pat dry with a clean towel. Repeat 3 times a week.
6. Neem has strong antibacterial properties that kill acne-causing bacteria, while sandalwood cools and calms inflamed skin.

Safety Notes:
- Perform a patch test on your inner wrist before applying to the face to check for sensitivity.
- Do not scrub or rub the skin when washing off the paste to avoid spreading bacteria.
