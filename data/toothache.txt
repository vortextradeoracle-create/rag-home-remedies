Remedy Name: Emergency Relief for Toothaches

Symptoms Covered: Sharp tooth pain, gum swelling, sensitivity to hot and cold.

Natural Ingredients: Whole Cloves (Laung), Clove Oil, Warm Water, Salt.

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Whole Clove Compression):
1. Place a whole clove (Laung) near the aching tooth.
2. Bite down gently on the clove to release its natural oils.
3. Keep it in place for 15 to 20 minutes. Cloves contain eugenol, a natural anesthetic and antibacterial agent that numbs the pain.

Remedy B (Clove Oil Application):
1. Dilute 1 to 2 drops of pure clove oil with a few drops of coconut oil.
2. Dip a clean cotton swab in the mixture and apply it directly to the affected tooth and gums.
3. Keep it there for 10 minutes, then spit. Do not swallow.

Remedy C (Warm Salt Water Rinse):
1. Mix half a teaspoon of salt in a glass of warm water.
2. Rinse your mouth thoroughly for 30 seconds and spit. This cleanses debris and disinfects the area.

Safety Notes:
- Clove oil is very strong; always dilute it as undiluted oil can irritate or burn sensitive gum tissue.
- These remedies are temporary. Consult a dentist to treat the root cause of tooth decay or infection.
