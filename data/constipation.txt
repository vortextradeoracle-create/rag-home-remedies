Remedy Name: Natural Remedies for Constipation and Bowel Regularity

Symptoms Covered: Hard stool, infrequent bowel movements, mild abdominal bloating.

Natural Ingredients: Clarified butter (Ghee), Warm Milk or Water, Triphala powder.

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Ghee and Warm Milk):
1. Heat one cup of milk (or water if lactose intolerant) until warm.
2. Stir in one teaspoon of pure cow ghee.
3. Drink this mixture warm just before bedtime.
4. Ghee acts as a natural lubricant for the intestinal lining, easing bowel movements the next morning.

Remedy B (Triphala Powder):
1. Mix half a teaspoon to one teaspoon of Triphala powder (an Ayurvedic mixture of three fruits: Amla, Haritaki, and Bibhitaki) in a cup of warm water.
2. Let it sit for 5 minutes and drink it before sleeping.
3. Triphala acts as a gentle, non-habit-forming laxative that cleanses the colon.

Safety Notes:
- Ensure you drink at least 8 to 10 glasses of water daily, as fiber and lubricants require hydration to work.
- If constipation persists for more than a week, consult a healthcare provider.
