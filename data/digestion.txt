Remedy Name: Natural Remedies for Indigestion and Gas

Symptoms Covered: Bloating, stomach ache, gas, heaviness after meals.

Natural Ingredients: Carom seeds (Ajwain), Fennel seeds (Saunf), Buttermilk (Chaas), Ginger juice, Black Salt (Kala Namak).

Method and Preparation:
Remedy A (Ajwain and Black Salt Chew):
1. Take half a teaspoon of carom seeds (Ajwain) and mix it with a pinch of black salt.
2. Chew this mixture thoroughly and swallow it with a glass of warm water.
3. Ajwain contains thymol, which stimulates gastric juices and relieves bloating and spasms quickly.

Remedy B (Digestive Buttermilk):
1. Mix one cup of fresh, plain yogurt with one cup of water and whisk thoroughly to make buttermilk.
2. Add a pinch of roasted cumin powder (Jeera), a pinch of black salt, and a few chopped mint leaves.
3. Drink this buttermilk after lunch. It introduces healthy probiotics and cools the stomach, easing digestion.

Remedy C (Fennel Seed Water):
1. Soak one teaspoon of fennel seeds (Saunf) in a cup of hot water for 10 minutes.
2. Strain and sip the water. Fennel seeds relax the gastrointestinal tract muscles, helping release trapped gas.
